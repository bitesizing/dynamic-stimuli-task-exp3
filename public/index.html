Study aims

This study was conducted as a way of investigating visual working memory - the ability to hold in mind visual features such as orientation, colour, and shape. The purpose of this research is to better understand the nature of memory storage and how it changes over time. Specifically, this study was looking at dynamic visual working memory, the ability to hold in mind information about moving or changing objects.
